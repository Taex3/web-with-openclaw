NovaCRM Prototype

Centralize customers, pipeline, and follow-ups in one clear workspace.

This prototype is designed for sales teams that currently track customers in Excel, Google Sheets, Line Chat, and notebooks. It combines customer records, opportunity tracking, dashboard analytics, priority alerts, and simple action guidance in a single SaaS-style CRM.
